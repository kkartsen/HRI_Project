Study on Camera Placement for Teleoperation

Welcome to the Camera Placement Study!

In this study, you will be teleoperating a robot in a simulated environment. Your task is to pick up an object and place it into a bin while using different camera angles. After each trial, you will be asked to fill out a survey about your experience with that camera angle.

User controls:

W: Moves the robot arm forward
S: Moves the robot arm backward
A: Moves the robot arm left
D: Moves the robot arm right
Q: Moves the robot arm up
E: Moves the robot arm down

Please note all controls are relative to the robot, not the camera.

This study is part of a research project on human-robot interaction by students in RBE 526 at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.
